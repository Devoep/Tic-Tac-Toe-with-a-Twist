How to play

Take turns on the same device. Click a cell to place your symbol. First to get 3-in-a-row wins — but every round a random chaos rule kicks in!

  • Normal Classic rules. No twist — recover and plan.
  • Swap! Every 2 moves, all X's and O's on the board swap symbols. Your winning line can flip instantly!
  • Vanish You can only hold 3 pieces at once. Placing a 4th removes your oldest.
  • Blocked cell One random cell is sealed — no one can play there this round.
  • Bomb move Each player has one bomb. Instead of placing, click an opponent's piece to erase it. Once per game.
  • Speed round You have 5 seconds per move — or your turn is skipped!

The score persists across rounds. Keep playing to see who dominates the chaos!
